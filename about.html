About me

Junior Web Developer
and Photographer

Hello there stranger,

My name is Lars de Jonge, a young junior web developer that is just over twenty-four springs young, living in The Netherlands. I studied web development at Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht and Communication and Multimedia Designer at Hogeschool Utrecht.

My love for visual design started when I was around five while playing with Legos every single day creating structures. Growing up I was fascinated by other people creating visual designs online. The interest inspired me to start experimenting and creating my own visuals with the help of some YouTube tutorials.

I wanted to display my work somewhere but didn’t know how or where. Figuring out where to display my work, created an interest towards web development. I started creating websites with WordPress and using website builders to show off my work & creations. My interest in web development grew more into the front-end side. I liked playing around with design elements and layouts of websites, instead of creating full-on applications. After finishing high school I decided to pursue web development and eventually graduated with a degree in that field.

Those are just the few things about me!

What Do I Bring To The Table
Web Development

Web development has a broad spectrum of things where you can develop yourself into. While graduating at Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht I figured out that I wanted to develop myself more on the front-end side. I love seeing code you create come to life. Front-end gives me the ability to combine my passion for design with HTML 5 & CSS 3. Creating this portfolio was mostly front-end development, but because I wanted some specific functionalities I came across JavaScript. Coding in this new language made me really interested in it, because it was a lot of problem solving which I like. Now I’m currently expending my knowledge of JavaScript & React with courses in my free time.

UI/UX Design

One of the most important aspects besides the product design is the usability and the user experience of the product. In my UI/UX specialization at Hogeschool Utrecht we learned how the users will react to certain obstacles and flows of a product. Using Adobe XD I can focus more on the behavior of the users rather than focusing on the design instead. With the help of different UX methods, I can pinpoint the problems of the users. This knowledge helps me to build a more meaningful product that fits the consumer’s needs.

Web Design

The love I have for front-end doesn’t stopped by coding a website for me. Another thing that I love doing is creating the design of a website. Trying to trigger the users to stay on the website for a longer periods of time or by trying to create more conversion is an art within itself. To structure the web design I use my experience from my UI/UX design specialization and create sketches. After the sketches I use tools such as Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator to create a more professional looking website.

Graphic Design

Graphic design was the first digital creation tool that took my interest. It started off by creating primarily online visuals with Adobe Photoshop and the help from YouTube. I began expending my knowledge about graphic design and even started off creating offline visual designs for clients. With the help of Adobe InDesign I could create offline visuals for fairs and magazines. I have been doing graphic design now over 8 years on and off.

Photography

There is no other feeling in the world like capturing that perfect moment while being outside in nature or surrounded by friends. Photography is a creative outlet to express my creative view by telling a story with just a few photos, while also capturing a memory that will last on forever. A tool to help with my creative outlet is Adobe Lightroom. Lightroom helps me maintain a consistent style throughout all of my pictures.

Video Editing

A good story can remind you back to the first time you heard or seen it. That is why a big side interest of mine is creating videos. It gives me the ability to create a story and show others how I see it. Most of the videos were created in Sony Vegas, Adobe Premiere & Adobe Effects. Besides visuals do you need the right sound to let your story come to life. Therefor I used sound recordings created or edited in Adobe Audition.
